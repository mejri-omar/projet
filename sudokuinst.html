Sudoku Game Instructions

Objective

The objective of Sudoku is to fill a 9×9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3×3 subgrids that compose the grid (also called "boxes", "blocks", or "regions") contain all of the digits from 1 to 9.

How to Play

Follow these steps to play:

  • Look at the numbers already filled in the grid.
  • Each number can only appear once in each row, column, and 3×3 box.
  • Fill in the empty cells with the correct numbers.
  • If you get stuck, you can use hints or check your progress.

Tips

  • Start with the rows, columns, or boxes that have the most numbers filled in.
  • Use the process of elimination to narrow down your options.
  • Take your time and enjoy the challenge!

Enjoy the Game!
