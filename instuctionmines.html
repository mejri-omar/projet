Objective

Your goal is to clear a grid containing hidden "mines" without detonating any of them. You can reveal squares in the grid and use the numbers revealed to deduce the locations of the mines.

How to Play

  1. Starting the Game: Click on any square to begin. The game will reveal that square and the surrounding squares.
  2. Understanding the Numbers: Each number represents the count of mines in the adjacent squares (including diagonals).
  3. Clearing Squares: Click on squares to reveal them. If you reveal a mine, the game is over!
  4. Flagging Mines: Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag a suspected mine. This helps you keep track of where you think the mines are.
  5. Winning the Game: You win by revealing all squares that do not contain mines.

Tips

  • Start by clicking on the corners or edges of the grid to reveal larger areas.
  • Use logic to deduce where mines are based on the numbers you see.
  • Don't rush! Take your time to think through your moves.

Good Luck!

Have fun playing Minesweeper! May your clicks be safe!
